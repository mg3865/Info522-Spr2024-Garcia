      Introduction

      Inspired by the students of the University of Maryland at College Park, we designed a website to assist in traveling to the United States Capital. Explore our website, to find popular and hidden areas in the city that you would like to visit. We included monuments, memorials, and restaurants that are worth a trip. Search or browse and you'll be provided addresses, emails, and phone numbers to assist in your travels.
